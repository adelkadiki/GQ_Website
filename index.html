Architects & Designers

We understand that Architects and Designers are devoted to the planning, designing, and overseeing of projects. GQ Flooring understands the importance of staying current and in-tune with the trends of today. Since space and ambience need to reflect functional, technical, social, environmental, and aesthetic considerations; we strive to ensure that the flooring process is a seamless experience. From selection to installation – our team of friendly professionals will ensure that you are happy with the results. Through our membership with the BCFCA, we have access to current up-to-date Architectural Specification Guides – these guides let the General Contractor and Architect know how to proceed with an installation. It is strongly recommended that Consultants use these Specification Guides for their project flooring specification requirements.

Restoration & Insurance

GQ Flooring works closely with many restoration project managers serving Vancouver and the Lower Mainland. We are a preferred ICC member and proudly support both the online quoting and invoicing systems. We value the importance of ICC guidelines for insurance and restoration companies. We will also attend service calls for wood repairs, touch-ups, board replacements, carpet stretching, seaming, binding custom rugs, re-grouting tile, cleaning grout lines, and sealing natural stones. We can also accommodate after hours work, and dispatch more than one crew to comply with any kind of urgent deadline. We carry a wide variety of flooring options from most major manufactures both ICC approved and independent. You can either visit one of our showrooms by appointment or we can go to your home or place of work and bring flooring samples to you.
